# 📊 Target Business Analysis using SQL

## Project Overview

This project analyzes a large-scale Brazilian e-commerce dataset using **Google BigQuery SQL** to uncover business insights across sales, customer behavior, regional performance, payment methods, and logistics operations. The objective was to transform raw transactional data into actionable recommendations that support data-driven business decisions.

---

## Business Problem

An e-commerce company wanted to better understand its sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, logistics efficiency and regional growth opportunities. The analysis focuses on identifying trends, operational bottlenecks and high-value markets to support strategic planning and business expansion.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze sales performance and growth trends.
- Evaluate customer distribution and purchasing behavior.
- Identify high-performing and under-penetrated markets.
- Assess payment preferences and installment behavior.
- Measure logistics and delivery performance.
- Generate actionable business recommendations.

---

## Dataset

- **Domain:** E-commerce
- **Platform:** Google BigQuery
- **Data Includes:**
  - Customers
  - Orders
  - Order Items
  - Payments
  - Sellers
  - Products
  - Delivery Information

---

## Key Business Insights

- Identified strong year-over-year growth and seasonal purchasing patterns.
- Uncovered revenue concentration in major states while highlighting high-value, low-volume regions with growth potential.
- Evaluated payment preferences and installment usage to understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Analyzed delivery timelines and logistics performance to identify operational bottlenecks.
- Generated business recommendations to improve regional expansion, marketing effectiveness, and logistics efficiency.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Increase marketing investment in high-value emerging markets.
- Optimize logistics operations in regions with longer delivery times.
- Plan inventory around seasonal demand fluctuations.
- Strengthen customer acquisition strategies in under-penetrated regions.
- Improve operational efficiency using data-driven performance monitoring.
